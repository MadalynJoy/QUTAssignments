Tic Tac Toe Help

    Select if the game will be Human vs Human, or Human vs Computer.
    If Human vs Computer mode is selected, the human will always be X.

Tic Tac Toe Rules:

    - Tic Tac Toe is made by creating a 3x3 grid.
    - Players take turns placing one marker at a time (an X or an O).
    - To win, a line of three of your tile piece must be made (e.g. A vertical line, a horizontal line, or a diagonal line all of Xs).
    - A game can be considered a draw if no matching lines are made and all 9 spaces are filled.
